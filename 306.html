This recursion quickly becomes expensive. If you uncomment line
[1]
, you’ll discover
that it takes far too long to complete, and overflows the stack while making all those
recursive calls. If
sum(100_000)
still works on your machine, try a bigger number.
When you call
sum(100_000)
, you add
100_000 sum()
function calls to the call
stack, causing a
StackOverflowError
. For comparison, line
[2]
uses the
sum()
library function to add the numbers within the range, and this does not fail.
To avoid a
StackOverflowError
, you can use an iterative solution that loops rather
than making recursive calls:
