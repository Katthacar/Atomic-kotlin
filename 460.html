Logging implementations that log to files often manage those log files by automati-
cally discarding the oldest parts when files get too large. There are additional tools
designed to read and analyze log files. The practice of logging can require fairly
involved research.
For basic problems, the work of installing, configuring, and using a logging system
might tempt you back to
println()
statements. Fortunately, there are easier
strategies.
