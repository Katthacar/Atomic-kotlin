Different kinds of nullability annotations are supported, using different names:
•
@Nullable
and
@CheckForNull
are specified by the JSR-305 standard.
•
@Nullable
and
@NonNull
are used in Android.
•
@Nullable
and
@NotNull
are supported by JetBrains tools.
• There are others. You can find the full list in the Kotlin documentation.
