Because of evaluation order, it doesn’t. Kotlin first divides

Understanding evaluation order helps you to decipher what a program does, both
with logical operations (Boolean expressions) and with mathematical operations. If
you’re unsure about evaluation order, use parentheses to force your intention. This
also makes it clear to those reading your code.
