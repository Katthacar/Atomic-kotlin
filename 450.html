assert()
To avoid commenting and uncommenting
check()
statements,
assert()
allows
you to enable and disable
assert()
checks.
assert()
comes from Java. Assertions are disabled by default, and are only engaged
if you explicitly turn them on using a command-line flag. In Kotlin, this flag is
-ea
JVM
.
We recommend using
require()
and
check()
, which are always available without
special configuration.
