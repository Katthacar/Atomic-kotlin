Operators can improve the experience of working with special libraries, so they
are primarily overloaded by library writers. The exception, and the operator that
might most often be found in custom classes, is
compareTo()
. If you implement the
Comparable
interface and override its
compareTo()
, you automatically provide all
basic comparison operations:
